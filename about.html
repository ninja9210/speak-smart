About Speak Smart Academy

Our Story

Speak Smart Academy was founded by a team of industry veterans who noticed a significant gap in the professional world: millions of talented, skilled individuals were being held back simply because they couldn't communicate their ideas effectively.

We saw brilliant engineers struggle in client meetings, innovative managers fail to inspire their teams, and bright graduates freeze in their first job interview. We decided to build the solution.

Our mission is to democratize high-quality communication training, making it practical, accessible, and affordable for every Indian aspiring to grow.

Our Core Values

Practicality First

No outdated theory. Our curriculum is 100% focused on real-world scenarios, from acing interviews to delivering board-level presentations.

Confidence Building

We believe communication is 20% technique and 80% confidence. Our mentors are trained to be your biggest supporters.

Accessibility

Just like PW revolutionized tech education, we are making premium, executive-level coaching available to everyone, everywhere.

Personalized Feedback

You can't improve what you don't measure. We provide 1-on-1, actionable feedback in a safe and supportive environment.
